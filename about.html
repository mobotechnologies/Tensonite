About

We are the best Design, Engineering & Fabrication

Tensonite is specialized in the installation of custom based tensile structures and Pre Fabricated Structures.A Trendsetter in Tensile Fabric Structures. Tensile fabric structures are increasingly becoming a common sight in india.Tensonite believes in best quality timely de;ivery at an affordable cost.Earlier know as Everest Tent Technologies,which has beeb a leader in the domain since 1992 and now incorporated as TENSONITE PRIVATE LIMITED.

Tensonite understands the key requisites of Clients- in demand of highly technical tensile structures ,design ,schedule,and price. Our business has been successfully from proven performance in single source responsibility from concept to completion of installation. Our commitment to client satisfaction goes beyond project completion. Maintenance support is an integral part of Tensonite.

Combining expert design skills and onsite knowledge enables the company to provide efficient and economical solutions for all types of tents and tensile structures. The dedicated inhouse erection team of the company has already done several successful installations. The company offers a wide range of products including Tents and Tensile Structures,Domes,Biogas plant a complete solutions with fabric, Marquee with full aluminium structure in higher span,auditorium and amphitheatre structures , and pre fabricated mobile office. Among its latest product offerings are ETPF and relief tent[HDPE tarpaulins].
